Gentrification
I had been a victim of gentrification. At a very young age, my immigrant family decided to move out of the Bay Area into a more financially stable area. By the time I had moved back to attend college, it had changed completely due to further gentrification. The residents in areas like the Mission had been completely pushed out by tech companies, business men, and the memory of all the immigrant families that had once viewed this place as home.
I decided to do this project in an attempt to call attention to the gentrification problem in San Francisco and put myself as the gentrified to bring light to a personal experience.
