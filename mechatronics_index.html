At Stanford University, Maribel worked on a team of four people to design and build a robot that maneuvers through an arena to a loading pad where the robot was loaded with foam balls and then used sensors to locate a target and launch the balls at the target until it is taken down. This robot was built from scratch. The team designed and laser cut the structure of the robot, built and tested the circuitry and coded the functionality of the robot. It was a major project that was created within three weeks but was very well done. If you would like to know more about this project, check out the actual website she created for it!
