At Stanford University, Maribel worked on a team of four people to design and build an android application to help Stanfordâ€™s Resident Assistants(RA) plan and organize a Stanford traditional scavenger hunt in the beginning of every school year. The application had two different frameworks, an organizer side and a player side. On the organizer side, the RAs can pick challenges from a premade list of challenges or make their own challenges, they can view information about all the teams, they can approve or reject student submissions, broadcast announcements, and view player rankings. On the player side, students can add members to their teams, they can view their ranking, check announcements, and chat with the organizers.
