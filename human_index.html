Human Engineers

Maribel went on a medical mission to the philippines to build prosthetic legs for amputee patients. The team met up on a weekly to learn anatomy of the leg, the different parts of the prosthetic leg and a variety of methods to fix and reuse material to have a more efficent mission. Most of the legs that were gifted to the patients were made of spare parts and were designed specifically for the patient's needs. Maribel was also able to assist in a fitting where we create the socket of the leg from scratch due to complications that enabled them to reuse material.
