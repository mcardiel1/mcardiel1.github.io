Digital Design

Maribel worked on a team of 3 people to implement a music box using a FPGA and monitor. Her team worked effortlessly to add multiple features to this music box such as playing multiple notes (chords) at a time, being able to display the past, future, and current notes on the display, showing the waveforms for the different notes that were playing and adding playback control such as rewind and fastforward.
